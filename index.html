Seismic — private, hardware-backed encryption at the base layer

Seismic integrates base-layer encryption with secure hardware to protect state across transitions. The stack is open-source and developer-friendly (seismic-revm, seismic-client). Built-in privacy makes it well-suited for private DeFi, confidential payments, and RWA tokenization.
